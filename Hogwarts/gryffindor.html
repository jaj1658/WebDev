On Running with the Lions

Gryffindor house is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was founded by Godric Gryffindor. Gryffindors are known for their nerve, chivalry and daring, although many view Gryffindor as an arrogant house. Members reside in the Gryffindor tower. According to J. K. Rowling, Gryffindor roughly corresponds to the element of fire.
